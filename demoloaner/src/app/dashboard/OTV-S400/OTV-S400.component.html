6 Auto-brightness adjustment Press the brightness mode button to set brightness mode to AUTO.
- Press the brightness adjustment button (“+” or “-“) to set the brightness level to “0.”
- Point the distal end toward an object. Move the distal end closer to the object and then away from it within a distance of 5 to 60 mm.
- Verify that the brightness of the endoscope image on the monitor is normal.
- Hold the distal end at a distance of 30 to 40 mm from the object.
- Press the brightness adjustment buttons (“+” and “-“). Inspect the following items:
・ When either of the brightness buttons is pressed, a beep sounds and the brightness increases or decreases.
・ When either of the buttons is pressed continuously, a beep sounds and the brightness indicator changes in a continuous manner.
- Verify that the light illuminated from the distal end increases or decreases according to the brightness setting.		 Pass Fail
